Privacy Policy

Awy is built around calm social connection, clear boundaries, and privacy-aware design. This policy explains what information Awy may collect, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Who Operates Awy

Awy is operated by Kyle D. Bousquet / LJB Media Group. LJB Brands may be used for public communication and support across Awy-related projects.

Contact: contact@ljbbrands.com

Age Requirement

Awy is for users who are at least 18 years old. You must be 18 or older to create or use an Awy account. Some Lounges may use a declared age range to help manage access, but declared age range is not government ID verification.

Information We May Collect

  • Account and profile basics, such as first name, optional last name, Awy Tag or handle, email address, birthday or age eligibility information, and profile details.
  • User content and activity, such as Strings, Lounges, Shared Areas, posts, messages, uploads, reactions, memberships, and related participation details.
  • Safety information, such as reports, blocks, moderation signals, abuse-prevention records, and support requests.
  • Device and app information, such as diagnostics, app usage signals, basic device data, and notification tokens if notifications are enabled.
  • Purchase-related information, if paid features are enabled. App Store purchases may be processed by Apple under Apple’s own policies.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide and sync the Awy experience, maintain accounts and profiles, support Strings, Lounges, Shared Areas, safety tools, notifications, customer support, abuse prevention, troubleshooting, and legal compliance.

Safety, Reports, and Moderation

Reports, blocks, and moderation-related information may be reviewed or retained to help protect users, investigate harmful behavior, prevent abuse, enforce rules, and comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • You can update profile information in the app where controls are available.
  • You can manage certain settings, visibility, and notifications in the app.
  • You can block or report users or content where safety tools are available.
  • You can delete or request deletion of your account from Settings.

Account Deletion

When you delete your Awy account from Settings, Awy is intended to remove the account and associated account data where required. Some information may be retained where necessary for safety, security, legal compliance, abuse prevention, dispute resolution, or backup integrity.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact contact@ljbbrands.com.
